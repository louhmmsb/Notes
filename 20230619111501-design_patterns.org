#+title: Design Patterns

Design patterns are typical solutions to common problems in software design.
They are divided in three major categories:
- Creational Patterns
- Structural Patterns
- Behavioral Patterns

The following site is a pretty good reference for some design patterns:
- https://refactoring.guru/design-patterns
